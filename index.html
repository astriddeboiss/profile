Some books I liked

The Confidant - Hélène Grémillon

The Confidant is an impressive blend of historical precision, high suspense and sharp-sighted psychological truths. A captivating novel with brilliant storytelling.

The Kite Runner - Khaled Hosseini

The Kite Runner (Les cerfs-volants de Kaboul) is the first novel by Afghan-American author Khaled Hosseini. Published in 2003 by Riverhead Books, it tells the story of Amir, a young boy from the Wazir Akbar Khan district of Kabul, whose closest friend is Hassan.

Stronger than hate: struggling to forgive - Tim Guenard

It is Tim Guenard's autobiography. Abandoned, beaten, left in foster care and placed in correctional facilities, surviving life on the streets, the author reveals how he got out of it.
